From our doorstep

Adventure when you want it, stillness when you need it.

Use Rockside Resort as your base to explore Cherrapunji’s living root bridges, caves and towering waterfalls — all within a short drive and trek from the property.

Double Decker Root Bridge, Nongriat

~11 km + 1.5–2 hour trek

Rare two-level living root bridge, over 200 years old. A must-do for trek lovers.

Mawmluh & Mawsmai Caves

20–25 min walk

Limestone passages, stalagmites and hidden pools carved by underground water.

Nohsngithiang & Nohkalikai Falls

7–8 km from resort

Seven Sisters Falls and India's tallest waterfall with sweeping cliff views.

Parks & Viewpoints

Eco Park, Thangkharang Park

Green spaces and viewpoints overlooking Bangladesh and Kynrem Falls.
