The Boho Life

More than a stay — an experience.

Every corner of Boho Studio is designed to slow you down and awaken your senses. From the hand-woven textiles to the clay pottery in the kitchen — here, life moves at the speed of chai.

🕯️
Evening Bonfire Circles Gather around the fire, share stories, stargaze. A tradition every night at Boho Studio.
🧘
Morning Yoga & Meditation Start your day grounded. Open-air sessions available every morning at sunrise.
🎨
Art & Craft Workshops Macramé, pottery, and natural dyeing workshops for those who want to create.
🌿
Herb Garden Walks Explore our kitchen garden, pick fresh herbs, and learn about natural remedies.
