Discover Guwahati

Assam's spirit in every experience.

From sacred temples to the mighty Brahmaputra's banks, from rich cultural heritage to vibrant local life — Guwahati's treasures are minutes away. Explore at your own pace.

~13 km drive
Kamakya Temple
One of the holiest Shakti shrines in India, perched on a hilltop with panoramic views of the Brahmaputra. Witness evening rituals and experience Assam's spiritual heritage.
~7 km drive
Umananda Island
The smallest river island in the world, offering a peaceful escape with stunning views. Perfect for an evening ferry ride and spiritual tranquility amid the river.
~8 km drive
Brahmaputra Heritage Centre
Explore Assam's rich cultural heritage through artifacts, textiles, sculptures, and tribal crafts. Understand the region's history and artistic traditions.
~25-30 mins by car
Brahmaputra Riverside Strolls
Walk along the banks of India's mightiest river at sunset. Visit riverside cafes, experience local life, or arrange evening river cruises for an unforgettable Brahmaputra experience.
